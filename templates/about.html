May Mois is a play on words. Mois is French for month. The name, then, writes me and my poetry into the context of the universe as a whole.

I am a
non-binary
they/them pronouns
college student, self-diagnosed with poetry.

In my poetry, I mosty look towards celebration or investigation. Celebration of life and its highs and lows is a concept dear to my heart. Not only the pleasures of life, but also its pains deserve to be understood and celebrated, though, it often happens in different ways.

On the other hand, investigation in my poetry often takes either a speculative or an introspective form. Other than language investigaton, which is another genre, investigation of the world for me involves looking within and looking out.

Language poetry, on the other hand, is more specific. It features an exploration of language as an always-evolving social construct. A lot of my poetry involves language exploration, but language poetry is specifically dedicated to this cause.
